Basic Vi commands
1. ESC :q (TO QUIT)
2. ESC :wq (TO SAVE AND QUIT)
3. ESC :w (TO WRITE)
4. v to select
5. y to copy the selected text
6. x to cut the selected text
7. p to paste the selected text
8. x to delete a character
9. u to undo 
10. ctr r to redo
11. i to insert or edit
